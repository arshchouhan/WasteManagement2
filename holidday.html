Holiday Schedule

The holiday-season results in a lot of waste for pickup and disposal. Eco Harbor offers Holiday Tree Pickup on your regular trash day.

Eco Harboe observes the following holidays:  
New Years Diwali Independence Day
Holi Eid Christmas Day
   

Residential Services Example:

A holiday falls on the Wednesday in the middle of the week. Monday and Tuesday collection for Residential Services continue as normal. Wednesday through Thursdays collection service has shifted one day. The following week returns to the regular scheduled collection service.
