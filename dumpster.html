3 Yard Dumpster

The 3-yard dumpster is ideal for small construction projects, household and garage clean-ups, or for tight building sites where a larger dumpster will not fit. Commonly seen in alleyways, behind apartment buildings, and servicing small businesses.

Approximate size (Excluding wheels): 4′ Tall x 3.5 Wide x 6′ Long

Maximum Weight limit: 800 pounds

*No household hazardous waste allowed.

10 Yard Dumpster

The 10-yard “lowboy” is ideal for concrete, brick, tile, dirt, and sand.

Acceptable materials include “inert” (meaning non-organic) materials:

  • Concrete
  • Dirt
  • Sand
  • Brick
  • Block
  • Tile

  • Clay tile
  • Ceramic tile
  • Clay tile
  • Stucco (no wire mesh)
  • Plaster

Maximum Weight Capacity: 10 tons / 20,000 Pounds

*No household hazardous waste allowed.

 

12 Yard Dumpster

The 12-Yard Roll-Off dumpster is ideal for the disposal of wood, plastic, green waste and household items.

Acceptable materials include: all types of non-hazardous construction and demolition (C&D) materials such as:

  • Lumber
  • Metal

  • Plastic
  • Drywall

Rate includes up to 3 tons/6,000 pounds.

*Should your dumpster weight exceed this limit, we may not be able to provide service.
**No dirt, rock, concrete, roofing, brick or ceramic tile.
**No hazardous waste (oil, car batteries, paint).
**No auto parts.

26 Yard Dumpster

The 26-yard dumpster is ideal for roofing projects.

Acceptable materials include all types of non-hazardous construction and demolition (C&D) materials such as:

  • Wood
  • Metal
  • Plastic
  • Drywall

  • Roofing Material
  • Furniture and Appliances
  • Green Waste

The approximate capacity of 10 pick up truck loads.

The maximum weight limit is 10 tons / 20,000 pounds

*No household hazardous waste allowed.

40 Yard Dumpster

The 40-yard dumpster is ideal for large remodels, construction projects, and cleanups.

Acceptable materials include all types of non-hazardous construction and demolition (C&D) materials such as:

  • Wood
  • Metal
  • Plastic
  • Drywall

  • Roofing Material (up to 75 squares of shingles)
  • Furniture and Appliances
  • Green Waste

Approximate capacity of 13 pick up truck loads.

Rate includes up to 6 tons or 12,000 pounds

Maximum weight limit is 6 tons/12,000 pounds

*No household hazardous waste allowed.
